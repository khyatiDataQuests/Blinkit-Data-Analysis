# 🛒 Blinkit Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end business performance analysis of **Blinkit**, one of India’s leading quick-commerce platforms. The objective of this analysis is to uncover meaningful insights from multiple business datasets related to orders, customers, delivery operations, products, customer feedback, and marketing campaigns. By combining these datasets, the project aims to evaluate overall business health and identify opportunities for growth, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

The analysis was conducted using **Python**, with key libraries such as **Pandas**, **NumPy**, **Matplotlib**, and **Seaborn** for data cleaning, transformation, visualization, and exploratory data analysis (EDA).

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

The primary goals of this project were:

- Analyze order volume, revenue trends, and average order values  
- Understand customer behavior, segmentation, and repeat purchase patterns  
- Evaluate delivery performance and its impact on customer satisfaction  
- Identify top-performing products and sales concentration  
- Measure marketing effectiveness through ROAS and campaign performance  
- Generate actionable business recommendations using data-driven insights  

---

## 📂 Datasets Used

This project integrates multiple datasets to simulate a real-world business environment:

- **Orders Dataset** – Order dates, order values, payment methods, delivery status, store information  
- **Order Items Dataset** – Product-level quantity and pricing details  
- **Customers Dataset** – Customer profiles, segments, total orders, spending behavior  
- **Delivery Dataset** – Delivery timing, delays, distance, partner performance  
- **Customer Feedback Dataset** – Ratings and feedback associated with orders  
- **Marketing Dataset** – Campaign spend, conversions, revenue generated, ROAS

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- A small number of high-value orders contribute significantly to overall revenue.  
- Customer acquisition shows steady growth over time.  
- Delivery delays are associated with lower customer ratings.  
- A few products dominate revenue generation, indicating product concentration.  
- Marketing campaigns deliver relatively stable ROAS with room for optimization.  

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Improve delivery efficiency to enhance customer satisfaction and ratings.  
- Retain high-value customers through loyalty programs and personalized offers.  
- Focus inventory and promotions on top-performing products.  
- Increase basket size through bundling and cross-selling strategies.  
- Optimize campaign targeting and budget allocation for better ROI.
